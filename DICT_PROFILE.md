## When to use PWK

Use PWK as a compact German-language alternative to PWG for quick lookups, or when PWG's 7-volume format is unwieldy. PWK adds corrections to PWG while abridging. For citation tracing, prefer PWG.

---

## Relationship to MW

PWK is Böhtlingk's condensed single-volume abridgment of [PWG](https://github.com/sanskrit-lexicon/PWG). With 170,556 records it is the largest CDSL dict by count; but citation density (0.515 ls/rec) is much lower than PWG (4.63) — condensation preferentially dropped citations. MW (1 vol, English) and PWK (1 vol, German) are useful counterparts for the same era.
